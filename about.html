My Story

A designer shaped by old libraries, candlelit evenings, and the belief that beauty is never frivolous.

grew up surrounded by books — not just the words inside them, but the smell of aging paper, the texture of cloth covers, the way old ink sits on a page. That love of the physical, of things made with care and intention, is at the heart of everything I create.

After studying Fine Arts and later teaching myself to code, I found myself in the rare position of speaking both languages: visual beauty and technical precision. I use both fluently, and I refuse to sacrifice one for the other.

"Every pixel is a brushstroke. Every line of code, a sentence in a story."

The Way I Work

I begin every project with research — not just into the client's needs, but into the history and aesthetics of the problem. What does this brand want to feel like? What era, what texture, what emotion? Only when I have that answer do I open my tools.

I work closely with clients at every stage, sharing sketches, moodboards, and prototypes long before delivering final files. The process is as important as the product.
